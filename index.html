ABOUT ME

Bridging DevOps reliability with cutting-edge AI research.

📍 Yogyakarta, Indonesia

I'm a Software Developer & DevOps Engineer with 3+ years of hands-on experience in full-stack development and containerized infrastructure, currently advancing as an S2 MKOM student at UGM — Indonesia's top research university.
